Blue Hawaiian

Blue Hawaiian: Tropical paradise in a glass with rum, coconut, and pineapple

Blue Hawaiian is a tropical and alluring cocktail that embodies the essence of a beach vacation in a single sip. This delightful drink blends the smoothness of rum with the sweetness of coconut cream, splashed with the vibrant flavors of pineapple juice and blue curaçao. Served over ice and garnished with a slice of juicy pineapple and a maraschino cherry, the Blue Hawaiian captivates with its mesmerizing azure hue. With a perfect balance of fruity and creamy notes, it transports you to a paradise of relaxation, making it an irresistible treat for any summer gathering or a moment of personal indulgence

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ oz (45 ml) light rum
  • 1 oz (30 ml) blue curaçao
  • 2 oz (60 ml) pineapple juice
  • 1 oz (30 ml) coconut cream
  • Ice cubes
  • Pineapple slice and maraschino cherry for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Fill a Shaker: Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes
  2. Add the Ingredients: Pour the light rum, blue curaçao, pineapple juice, and coconut cream into the shaker
  3. Shake: Securely close the shaker and shake vigorously for about 10-15 seconds to chill and thoroughly mix the ingredients
  4. Prepare the Glass: Fill a tall glass with ice cubes
  5. Strain and Pour: Strain the shaken cocktail into the prepared glass, ensuring that no ice or chunks of fruit make their way into the drink
  6. Garnish: Optionally, garnish the Blue Hawaiian with a slice of pineapple and a maraschino cherry for an extra tropical touch
  7. Serve and Enjoy: The Blue Hawaiian is now ready to enjoy! Sip and savor the tropical flavors of rum, blue curaçao, pineapple, and coconut cream that evoke the essence of a beautiful beach paradise
